mesosphere is a rapidly developing platform and we're always eager to hear your
feedback.

## Feedback and Support

Please share any general questions, feature requests, or product feedback in our
[community](https://www.usemesosphere.com/community). We're particularly
excited to see what you build on mesosphere!

Any specific support questions that aren't able to be adequately addressed on
our community channel can be directed to
[support@mesosphere.dev](mailto:support@mesosphere.dev).

## Following mesosphere

Release notes are shared on [mesosphere News](https://news.mesosphere.dev/tag/releases)
and the [community](https://www.usemesosphere.com/community).

Product announcements, articles and demos are posted on the mesosphere blog,
[News](https://news.mesosphere.dev/), [our YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCoC_9mdiPwIu1sDxDtGQggQ),
and [X (fka Twitter)](https://x.com/mesosphere).

## Vulnerability Disclosure

If you believe you've discovered a bug in mesosphere's security, please get in touch
at [security@mesosphere.dev](mailto:security@mesosphere.dev) and we'll get back to you
within 24 hours. We request that you not publicly disclose the issue until we
have had a chance to address it.
