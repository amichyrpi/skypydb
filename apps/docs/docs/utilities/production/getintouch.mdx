# Feedback and Discussion

You can share your feedback and engage with the community on our social media:

- [X (Twitter)](https://x.com/mesosphere-backend)
- [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/mesosphere-backend/)
- [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/)

You can also join discussions on GitHub via [Discussions](https://github.com/Ahen-Studio/mesosphere-backend/discussions).

# Reporting Issues and Vulnerabilities

If you encounter a bug or discover a security vulnerability, you can report it through GitHub. We will do our best to respond promptly.

**Important Guidelines**

- Do **not** submit trivial or AI-generated false issues.
- Misuse of the reporting system may result in being banned from future reporting.

Thank you for your understanding and for helping us improve Mesosphere.
