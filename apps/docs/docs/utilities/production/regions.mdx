title: "Regions"
---

Having your data close to your application is now essential for building fast apps. That’s why you may want to choose the location of your Mesosphere deployment.

Your backend and functions will run in the selected region.

# Available regions

We currently support:

- US East (N. Virginia)
- EU East (Paris)

At the moment, we do not plan to add additional regions.

# Selecting your region

You can select your region from the [Mesosphere dashboard](https://mesosphere.ahen-studio.com/dashboard) when creating a new project.

Be careful when choosing your region, as it **cannot be changed afterward**.

# Migration

If you need to change your database region, you can do so by:

1. Backing up your current database from the [Mesosphere dashboard](https://mesosphere.ahen-studio.com/dashboard)

2. Creating a new project in the desired region

3. Importing your backup into the new project

Follow the [Backup and Restore](../../database/backuprestore) guide for more details.
